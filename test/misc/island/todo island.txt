- Island game
-- coconuts (as sprites) fall from the coconut tree and agregate below it into the ground (as tiles);
-- a fish (as sprite, flipping vertical each second) appear on the side opposite from the player side;
-- theres a hungry timer (player is dead when zero counter);
-- player must eat the fish to add 1/2 of hungry timer, and a new fish appears on the other side of island;
-- player must avoid the falling coconuts (sprites), or die;
-- player can jump to skip the fallen coconuts (tiles) if theres no coconut above it;
-- player can take a coconut to put another place or fire it into the sea;
-- player must goes to each side of island to eat a fish;
-- player can drink the coconut to add 1/8 of the hungry timer.
